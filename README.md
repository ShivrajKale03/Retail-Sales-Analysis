# 🛒 Retail Sales & Invoice Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales and invoice data using SQL to uncover insights related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

The analysis focuses on transforming raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support decision-making in inventory planning, customer retention, and revenue optimization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total sales and transaction activity
- Identify repeat and high-value customers
- Evaluate product sales performance
- Track invoice activity
- Explore sales trends over time
- Generate business recommendations from data

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- SQL
- MySQL / PostgreSQL
- GitHub

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### Revenue Analysis

- Invoice count

### Customer Analysis
- Repeat customer identification
- High-value customer analysis

### Product Analysis
- Top-selling products
- Highest revenue-generating items
- Frequently purchased products

### Sales Trend Analysis
- Monthly sales trends
- Peak transaction hours
- Overall business activity trends

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Repeat customers contributed a major share of total revenue
- A small group of customers generated the highest sales
- Top-performing products consistently drove revenue
- Monthly trends showed fluctuations in sales activity
- Peak transaction hours highlighted the most active sales periods

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Build loyalty strategies for repeat customers
- Maintain inventory for high-demand products
- Schedule promotions during peak sales periods
- Monitor monthly trends for better planning
